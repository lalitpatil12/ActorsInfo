Biography

Vijay Devarakonda

Born: 9th May 1989 (Achampet, Telangana, India) Vijay is most known for his part as "Rishi" in the 2015 film 'Yevade Subramanyam', which he made his film debut at a young age following a spell in the theatrical industry. Because Vijay's father was an actor, it was only natural for him to pursue a career in the entertainment industry. He, like his father, discovered his interest in front of the camera and began to devote his time and energy to the business shortly after. While being born into the actor's family may have given Devarakonda an advantage, no one can dispute that he has innate talent and ability in the field. After making his theatrical debut, Devarakonda made a rapid transition to the silver screen, starring in many films. Devarakonda's major break came with the film 'Yevade Subramanyam', which followed his debut in a modest part.
